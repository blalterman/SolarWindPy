solarwindpy.instabilities.verscharen2016 moduleÔÉÅ

Instability thresholds from Verscharen et al. (2016).

The empirical fits of [] are implemented to evaluate when a plasma becomes unstable in the (beta, R) plane.

References

[1]

Verscharen, D., Chandran, B. D. G., Klein, K. G., & Quataert, E. Collisionless Isotropization of the Solar-Wind Protons By Compressive Fluctuations and Plasma Instabilities, Astrophys. J., 831, 128 (2016).

beta_ani_inst(beta, a=None, b=None, c=None)[source]ÔÉÅ

Return the anisotropy threshold for a given beta.

Implements Eq. (5) of []:

\[R_p = 1 + \frac{a}{(\beta_{\parallel,p} - c)^b}\]

where $p$ is defined assuming only a single proton population is fit.

Parameters:

  • beta (array-like) ‚Äì Parallel proton beta.

  • a (float) ‚Äì Fit parameters from Verscharen et al. (2016).

  • b (float) ‚Äì Fit parameters from Verscharen et al. (2016).

  • c (float) ‚Äì Fit parameters from Verscharen et al. (2016).

Returns:

Threshold anisotropy values.

Return type:

numpy.ndarray

Examples

>>> beta = np.logspace(-2, 2, 5)
>>> beta_ani_inst(beta, a=0.367, b=-0.408, c=0.011)  
array([       nan, 1.1367783..., 1.36534751..., 1.93857946..., 3.40240693...])
